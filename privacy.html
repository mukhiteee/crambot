Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

Welcome to CramBot! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our AI-powered study planning service.

TL;DR: We don't collect personal data. We don't sell anything. We use cookies for basic functionality. Your study data stays on your device.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide

When you use CramBot, you may provide:

  • Student name (optional)
  • Course names and credit units
  • Study preferences (hours per day, excluded days, study times)
  • Date ranges for your study schedule

‚úÖ Important: This data is processed locally in your browser and sent only to our AI service to generate your timetable. We do NOT store this data on our servers.

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect:

  • Cookies: Small files to remember your theme preference and rate limiting
  • Usage Data: Anonymous analytics about how users interact with CramBot (via Netlify Analytics)
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system (standard web server logs)

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Generate personalized study timetables using AI
  • Remember your preferences (theme, layout)
  • Prevent abuse through rate limiting
  • Improve CramBot based on anonymous usage patterns
  • Provide customer support (if you contact us)

‚úÖ We do NOT sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Third-Party Services

CramBot uses the following third-party services:

Groq API (AI Service)

Your course data is sent to Groq's AI service to generate timetables. Groq processes this data according to their privacy policy. We do not control Groq's data practices.

Cloudflare Workers (Hosting)

Our backend runs on Cloudflare Workers to protect our API keys and handle requests securely.

Netlify (Web Hosting)

CramBot is hosted on Netlify, which may collect basic analytics (page views, traffic sources).

4. Cookies and Local Storage

CramBot uses cookies and browser local storage for:

  • Theme Preference: Remembers if you prefer light or dark mode
  • Rate Limiting: Prevents abuse by limiting how often you can generate timetables
  • Cookie Consent: Remembers if you've accepted our cookie policy

You can clear cookies and local storage anytime through your browser settings. This will reset your preferences.

5. Data Security

We take security seriously:

  • All data transmission uses HTTPS encryption
  • We don't store your study data on our servers

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. Use CramBot at your own risk.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Clear cookies and local storage from your browser

To exercise these rights, contact us at: mukhiteee@gmail.com

7. Children's Privacy

CramBot is intended for students of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent. If you're under 13, please use CramBot with a parent or guardian.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of CramBot after changes means you accept the updated policy.
